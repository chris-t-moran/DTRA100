Triangle 100

History is inclined to focus on the extraordinary, but the extraordinary is only extraordinary. The ordinary, because it leaves no trace behind it, is precious.
Dermot Bolger (Ferguson Road, Sunday Press 1991)

Welcome to this celebration of one of Ireland's first social housing estates. In the lead up to 2028, the Drumcondra Scheme's centenary year, local citizen historians (or neighbours as they are also known!) continue the task of discovering more about our communities's rich history, its people and their ordinary lives.

Travel back in time to meet the first residents of 1929 and explore the stories and curiosities of 100 years of the Drumcondra Triangle, a place we are all proud to call home.
